Our Story

Webwise Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to democratize community building by making professional-grade membership platforms accessible to entrepreneurs and businesses of all sizes.

We recognized that many passionate community leaders were struggling with expensive, complex platforms that required technical expertise. Our founders, having built and managed multiple successful membership communities, decided to create a solution that anyone could use.

Today, ClubLaunch powers hundreds of membership communities worldwide, from fitness clubs and coaching programs to professional associations and creative collectives.
